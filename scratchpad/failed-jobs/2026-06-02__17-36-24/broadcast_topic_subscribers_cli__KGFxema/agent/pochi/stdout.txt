# Broadcast to Topic Subscribers with the MagicBell CLI ## Background MagicBell lets you fan a single broadcast out to every subscriber of a topic instead of having to enumerate recipients one by one. Passing the topic key as a bare string (e.g. `{ "topic": "topic-..." }`), or as a top-level recipient field, will be rejected and no notification will be delivered (see MagicBell Discussion #213).
